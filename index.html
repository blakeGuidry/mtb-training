Weekly Volume (hours)

Phase 3 Session A — Climbing Power & Unilateral Strength (Tue)

Protocol: 3 sets × 3–6 reps. Heavy (80–95% 1RM). Rest 2–4 min. Farmer's carries at end.

Exercise Sets Reps Load Why
Barbell Squat / Leg Press 3 3–6 Heavy Max leg force for climbing
Deadlift (or Trap Bar) 3 3–6 Heavy Posterior chain power, hip extension for pedaling
Pull-up / Lat Pulldown 3 3–6 Heavy Bar control on climbs and rough terrain
Bulgarian Split Squat 3 3–6/leg Heavy Unilateral strength — MTB is a single-leg sport. Protects against asymmetry over 7 race days
Farmer's Carry 3 40–60s Heavy Grip endurance, anti-lateral core, shoulder stabilization. Grip fatigue limits descending by Day 5+
Phase 3 Session B — Descending Resilience & Grip (Fri)

Protocol: 3 sets × 3–6 reps. Heavy. Rest 2–4 min. Wrist roller at end. Core routine follows.

Exercise Sets Reps Load Why
Barbell Squat / Leg Press 3 3–6 Heavy Foundational — repeated from Session A
Deadlift (or Trap Bar) 3 3–6 Heavy Posterior chain — repeated from Session A
Bent-over Row / Heavy DB Row 3 3–6 Heavy BCBR upper body demand is pulling: holding bars, absorbing impacts, climbing
Eccentric Step-down 3 8/leg Moderate 3–4s lowering off step. Eccentric quad strength for absorbing descent impacts. Prevents Day 5 jelly legs
Farmer's Carry 2 40–60s Heavy Grip and core endurance
Wrist Roller / Wrist Ext. Curls 2–3 Roll up/down Light Forearm extensors — prevents forearm pump and loss of brake control on tech descents

Protocol: 2 sets × 3–5 reps. Heavy but not to failure (~80% 1RM). Minimum effective dose.

ERG Mode Guide

ERG ON: All structured intervals — threshold, VO2 max, sweet spot, microbursts. Trainer auto-adjusts resistance to hold prescribed power.

ERG OFF: Free rides, recovery spins, climbing routes, and low-cadence torque work. You control effort via gearing/cadence — closer to outdoor feel.

Daily Race / Recovery Protocol

Execute after every stage to maximize next-day performance.

0–5 minFinish. Begin rehydrating immediately.
5–20 min15-min cool-down spin on flat ground. Must be Z1.
20–30 minRecovery nutrition: ~30g protein + ~60g carbs.
30–90 minRehydrate, stretch, foam roll, clean up. Fix any mechanicals.
2+ hoursFull balanced meal. Prep equipment for tomorrow. Maximize sleep.

HR-Based Intensity Cues (No Power Meter)

HR lags power by 20–30s. For intervals under 3 min, use RPE.

Zone % LTHR Feel
Z2 Endurance 69–83% Conversational. Full sentences.
Z3 Tempo 84–94% Short sentences, prefer not to talk.
Z4 Threshold 95–105% A few words at most. Labored breathing.
Z5 VO2 Max >106% Cannot talk. Counting down the seconds.
Z6 Anaerobic N/A Max effort, unsustainable beyond 30–60s.
